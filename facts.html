Ghast Sounds

Ghast sounds are made by C418's cats!

Lava flowing

In the Overworld, lava flows at 50% of the speed of water.

But in the Nether, it flows at 80%, so watch out!


Paused Overworld

In Minecraft, if all the players are in the Nether, the Overworld gets paused.

So crops don't grow, mobs don't move, and time doesn't pass, alongside other things.

Death Messages

When you die to an explosion, the death message "[Gamertag] was blown up by ______" will appear in the chat.

When you die to a melee attack, the death message "[Gamertag] was slain by ______" will appear in the chat.

When you die to a projectile, the death message "[Gamertag] was shot by ______" will appear in the chat.

When you burn to death, the death message "[Gamertag] burnt to a crisp" will appear in the chat.

When you die to magma, the death message "[Gamertag] discovered the floor was lava" will appear in the chat.

When you die to an exploding bed, the death message "[Gamertag] was slain by [Intentional Game Design]" will appear in the chat.

When you fly into a wall and die, the death message "[Gamertag] experienced kinetic energy" will appear in the chat.

When you fall to your death, the death message "[Gamertag] fell from a high place" will appear in the chat.

When you drown, the death message "[Gamertag] drowned" will appear in the chat.

When you die to potions, the death message "[Gamertag] was killed by magic" will appear in the chat.

When you fall into the void, the death message "[Gamertag] fell out of the world" will appear in the chat.

In some cases where there are other variants of the death message, we display the most common one.

For Bedrock edition only.
